DM NO. 032, S. 2023 | Submission of Notice Salary Adjustment (NOSA)

1. Relative to the National Budget Circular No. 591, dated January 10, 2023, effective January 01, 2023, this Office directs the field on the preparation of Notice of Salary Adjustment (NOSA) which shall be submitted on February 17, 2023.

2. In line with the preparation and submission of Notice of Salary Adjustment, the template is accessible through this link: http://bit.ly/3iYFa6y.

3. Further, it is expected that the records of employees in other agency shall also be updated. Hence, the proof of GSIS ARA shall be attached together with the NOSA.

4. Attached is a copy of the Memorandum for the reference of all concerned.
5. For relevant queries, you may contact the Personnel Unit at (046) 4237934/ 0919 060 5822. 6. Immediate dissemination of this memorandum is directed.
